SpliTip

The goal of this bootcamp project is to design a mobile app that solves a real need in a human’s day to day life.

SpliTip is an app that helps tipped employees calculate and split their final shift tip out. An automated system that calculates tips, logs in hours by shift, and connects co-workers at the same time.

Overview

At the end of their shift, tipped employees struggle to calculate the accumulation of tips made and then split it with the rest of their co- workers. The process is long and outdated, they write down their calculations on a sheet of paper to finally send it to a reviewer. This causes miscalculations, underpayments, delays, and miscommunication/conflicts within employees.


My Role: UX/UI Designer

Type: Group Project

Time: 2 Weeks

Problem

Tipped employees struggle to calculate their earned tips by the end of every shift. There is no convenient access to the calculations so they cannot verify the total payment for any of their previous shifts. How might we improve communication and transparency on this process while avoiding miscalculations and workplace conflicts.

Solution

We designed SpliTip app so that can help tipped employees calculate their tips automatically without any further calculation at the end of the shift. The app has a calendar to view previous, current and future shift and also provides access to payment history. Messaging feature is also accessible in the app for a better communication with co-workers and managers.

Interview

Our objective is to understand the tips calculation process, employees’ experience, and get their feedback and feelings about this process.
I order to achieve our research objectives and better understand users’ experiences and pain points, we’ve interviewed four (4) tipped employee.

Eduardo

“I need a tool that we and the managers could get access to, this could help to be more transparent and to store the information for taxes reasons”

Natalia

“I don't calculate my tips, I just receive them by the end of the month. It takes so long to calculate them and the manager calculate them for everyone and we have no tools or way to verify that the tips are accurate.”

Wireframes

User Testing

Our objective is to know how users feel when navigating through the app and find the main features to perform the required tasks.

Tasks

  • Start a new shift, add a tip and end the shift.
  • View past, current and future job schedule in the calendar.
  • Chat with co-workers and manager.
  • Search for payment report and share the file.

Feedback

  • Use liked the edit option to update the tip amount.
  • User liked the chat feature to communicate internally.
  • User was able to perform all the tasks easily and successfully.

Conclusion

During our project phases, we started from understanding user’s pain points and worked on potential solutions to help resolve their issues.

Our app is a combination of features based on users experience and feedback, which those features include tips calculation, calendar schedule, payment history and chat room.
